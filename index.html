Income Tax Calculator

Calculate your Income Tax

  • The calculator is per person.
  • The currency of the taxable income band is in Nigerian Naira. Convert any foreign income to local currency before using this calculator.
  • The calculator assume a deduction of 8% of the total income to the individual pension contributory.
  • The calculator assume a 2.5% to the NHF.
  • The calculator assume a 5% to the NHIS
  • The calculator also assume a consolidated relief fund of NGN200,000 per annum or 1% of annual gross income, plus 20% of annual gross income.
